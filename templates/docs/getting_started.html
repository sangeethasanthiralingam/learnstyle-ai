  • Safari 14+

Common Issues

Quiz not loading?

Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. Ensure JavaScript is enabled.

Can't see my results?

Make sure you've completed all 15 questions. Check your internet connection and try again.

Dashboard looks different?

Your dashboard adapts to your learning style. The layout and content will be personalized for you.
